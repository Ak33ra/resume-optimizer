# Leadership, Activities & Volunteering

> Copy to `VOLUNTEERING.md` and fill in. Include **only** items with real
> leadership, ownership, or measurable impact — a title plus a result. Passive
> membership ("member of X club") is filler and won't make the resume.

## Leadership
- **Role** — organization — dates
  - what you led, the scale (members, budget, events), and the outcome/impact
  - e.g. "President, ACM chapter (300 members) — grew attendance 3×; ran a 200-person hackathon"

## Teaching / mentoring
- TA / peer tutor / bootcamp mentor — course/program, term, scale (students helped)
  (if it was a paid/formal role, it can also live in `EXPERIENCE.md`)

## Open-source / community
- Maintainer/contributor role, community organizing, meetups/talks — with links

## Volunteering
- Cause/organization — your role — time commitment — tangible contribution
